About Glazur

Paris-designed womenswear — natural fabrics, limited editions, the same spirit as glazurshop.ru, in English.

Glazur is a brand for women who believe that everyday life can be beautiful. We create clothing that transforms ordinary days into small moments of magic: soft fabrics, thoughtful fit, and refined details make a woman feel confident, graceful, and inspired.

Our pieces are designed in Paris and crafted with careful attention to every detail. We release limited editions, where each item is unique and features a signature touch — a Glazur Detail, a small element that makes the look truly special.

We use only natural fabrics — cotton and linen — because we believe in beauty you can touch and feel. Lace, embroidery, delicate bows, and fine finishes create a sense of quiet luxury and gentle transformation: from tired and busy to inspired and graceful.

Glazur is not about an outfit, it’s a state of being. Every day in our clothing feels more beautiful, and the subtle admiration of those around you confirms it: you stand out gracefully, feminine, and uniquely. This is for women who want to feel exceptional, here and now.
