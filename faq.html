Yes. Complimentary standard shipping applies to U.S. orders over $250 USD (before tax). Under $250 USD, standard shipping is a flat $10 USD. Orders ship from Boca Raton, Florida, typically processed in 1–3 business days.

Yes. Free international shipping on orders over $400 USD; below that, rates are calculated at checkout. International orders are typically sent via DHL Express (about 3–7 business days). Duties and taxes may apply.

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags attached. Start a return by emailing info@glazurshop.com with your order number and return tracking number.

Store credit for the full item value, or refund to the original payment method with a $10 return fee. Original shipping is non-refundable. Returns are processed within 7–10 business days after we receive the item.

U.S. customers may request size exchanges subject to availability — email info@glazurshop.com. International exchanges are not available per your policy draft.

Hand washing in cold water with a gentle natural detergent is recommended for delicates. Machine wash cold on delicate when appropriate; air dry when possible. See Garment care for full instructions.

Garments are designed with a natural, feminine silhouette and typically run true to size. If between sizes, consider sizing up. Cotton and linen have little stretch — use our Size guide or email for help.
