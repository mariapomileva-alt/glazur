Size guide

From Size guide.docx. Reference for layout: RIXO London size guide (link in your source doc).

We are obsessed with fit and want you to feel your best, no matter what you’re dressing for. Every style is perfected by our in-house garment tech team until we’re happy that each seam sits exactly right. Our product pages include specific sizing information; the following is a general guide. If you need further assistance, email Customer Care.

How to measure

To ensure each piece fits beautifully and you feel confident and comfortable, please take your measurements carefully. Use a soft measuring tape and avoid pulling too tightly.

1. Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust while wearing the bra you would normally wear with this garment. Keep the tape horizontal and do not pull too tight.

2. Waist

Measure around your natural waist — the narrowest part of your torso, just below your rib cage.

3. Hips

Measure around the widest part of your hips. Do not wear shapewear, even if you would normally wear it with the garment. Keep the tape level all the way around.

4. Garment length — skirts

Measure from the center of your waist down the front of the skirt to the desired length.

5. Garment length — dresses, tops, blouses, jackets

Measure from the point where your neck meets your shoulder down the front of the garment to the hem.

Glazur tip: If you’re unsure about your size, contact us at info@glazurshop.com — we’re happy to help you find your perfect fit.
